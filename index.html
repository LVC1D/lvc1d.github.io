Who we are

Our Mission Statement

Our mission at Subiro is to pioneer the advancement of space technology, revolutionizing humanity's access to the cosmos. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation, enabling sustainable space exploration, and contributing to the development of a multi-planetary civilization.

Core Values

  1. Innovation: We drive progress through creative solutions.
  2. Collaboration: Together, we achieve space's next frontier.
  3. Sustainability: Responsible practices for Earth and beyond.
  4. Excellence: Quality and safety define our work.
  5. Inclusivity: Diverse perspectives power our success.

Milestones achieved

  • First Commercial Lunar Landing: First commercial landing on the Moon.
  • Launch Cost Reduction: Reduced costs for space access.
  • Advanced Satellite Constellations: Enhanced global communication.
  • Space Habitat Prototype: Prototype for future human habitation.
  • Sustainable Propulsion Systems: Minimizing space debris, eco impact.
